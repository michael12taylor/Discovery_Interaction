Josh Brolin took inspiration from Marlon Brando's performance as Colonel Kurtz in Apocalypse Now in his portrayal of Thanos. Christopher Markus stated in the audio commentary for Avengers: Infinity War that there were earlier drafts of the script spent more time on Titan before its downfall. Concept art sketched by Ryan Lang featuring a young Thanos and his Eternals family further indicates a that a flashback of Thanos' youth was considered to appear in the film. It also hints at the possibility of Thanos appearing in the upcoming Eternals film. Robert Pralgo was a reader for the role of Thanos in Avengers: Infinity War and Avengers: Endgame. Greg Rementer, Keil Zepernick, and Carmelo Oquendo were stunt doubles for Josh Brolin in the role of Thanos.
