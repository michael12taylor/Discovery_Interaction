Behind the Scenes The photo used for baby Fitz and his mother is actually Iain De Caestecker's real-life childhood photo of him and his mother, Linda De Caestecker. The relationship between Fitz and Jemma Simmons is based on the relationship between Jed Whedon and Maurissa Tancharoen. Fitz's obsession with monkeys was inspired by Iain De Caestecker's real-life love of monkeys. Due to his popularity, Leo Fitz was introduced into the main Marvel Comics universe. An alternate version of Fitz also appeared on the animated show Ultimate Spider-Man, voiced by Iain De Caestecker. Justin Gant, Josh Fried, and Tim Mikulecky were stunt doubles for Iain De Caestecker in the role of Leo Fitz.
